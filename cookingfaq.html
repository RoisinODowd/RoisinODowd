Cooking the World FAQ

  • How do you choose which country to cook next?
    • I choose the country based on what produce is in season, what spices/condiments I find at the grocery store, and what I'm in the mood to eat!
  • Why cook only one dish per country?
    • I normally cook for one person, making one or two dishes to eat. Sometimes I make more dishes to have a full meal, but as a student I don't usually have time to make multiple recipes at once.
  • How authentic is the food you make?
    • I aim to prepare the food as authentically as possible. I love experimenting with spices and bringing elements of other cuisines into my everyday cooking, but I don't count any 'fusion' recipes into the total countries cooked.
  • Did you know that _____ food isn't from one country? Why did you count _____ food for _____ country??
    • Yes I am aware, but in the end I need to decide under which country to count a food. If I count _____ food for a certain country, it's usually because I have recipes lined up for other countries in the region.
  • How does cooking relate to software development?
    • Even developers have to eat!
