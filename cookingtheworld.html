Cooking the World

One dish from each country

I've always loved to cook. As a college student, I decided to challenge myself and experience more cuisines by making food from every country in the world. I aim to learn a little about each country as I plan and prepare each meal - listening to some popular music, learning a few words in the country's language, and researching the history and culture of the country.

I choose countries based on seasonal produce and what I'm in the mood to eat. Now I'm 15 countries in and counting. Follow along as my tastebuds travel the globe!



Countries cooked:

  • Ireland: leek and potato soup, brown bread, coddle, scones
  • France: radish sandwiches, quiche
  • Spain: espinadas con garbonzos
  • Germany: brezeln, spaetzle
  • Israel: falafel, Israeli salad
  • Thailand: green curry
  • Tajikistan: osh, Tajik salad
  • United States: apple pie, chili, blueberry pancakes, and more!
  • Italy: pasta e cece
  • Russia: Russian tea with jam
  • Lebanon: mujadara
  • Ethiopia: misr wot
  • China: hot and sour soup, egg drop soup
  • Greece: greek chicken, tzatziki, Greek salad, pita
